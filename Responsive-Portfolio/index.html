About Me

Enjoy working at the Cleveland Clinic within the Education Institute and providing service towards the "further education of those who serve" portion of our triparted mission. Currently I hold the role of Director, Financial Operations and prior to that I was the Operations Manager of the Center for Continuing Education for a number of years. I hold a master's degree in Accounting and Financial Management and have plans to sit for the CPA exam.


I am currently a student of the Case Western Reserve University Coding Bootcamp to help further my knowledge on full stack development. Technology has been and will continue to be the wave of the future, especially in healthcare. On a daily basis we use and depend on multiple applications with many more in the pipeline. I plan on applying everything I learn in the Bootcamp to help the Cleveland Clinic Education Institute leverage our applications better, create more innovative technology solutions, as well as integrate multiple systems together to create greater enterprise efficiencies.
